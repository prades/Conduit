ENVIRONMENTAL HAZARDS
Hazards appear on floor tiles in zones beyond home. Watch your step.
Acid Pool — Glowing green puddles. Deals 3 dmg every 0.75s to any actor standing on it. Player takes 4 dmg. Covers 2–3 tiles.
Live Cable — Frayed wires stretching from wall cracks to the floor. Arc cycles: ON for ~0.7s then OFF for 1.5–3s. While live, deals 5 dmg every 0.25s to nearby actors, 6 dmg to player. Watch the sparks.
Element Triangles — two rock-paper-scissors loops. Strong = 1.3× dmg, Weak = 0.7× dmg. Ice vs Core = 0× (completely blocked).
⬡ VOLATILE Fire > Flux > Toxic > Fire
⬡ ARCANE Electric > Core > Ice > Electric
Fire & Electric start unlocked. Others purchased in the shop between waves.
FIRE Proc: 30% · Unlocked by default
Physical — Ring of Fire: 0.4× ATK dmg to all enemies within 1.5 tiles. Also hits nearby nests for 2× bonus damage.
Special — Meteor: 0.9× SP.ATK dmg in 2.0-tile radius at target. Deals 2× damage to nest pods within 3.0 tiles.
Ultimate — Meteor Burst: 2s cast. Map darkens, reticle locks the highest-HP enemy. On impact: 3.0× SP.ATK dmg in 5.0-tile radius (falls off with distance). Leaves a flame crater that deals lingering fire DoT for ~10s.
Burn proc (30%): DoT for ~2s on physical hits, ~3s on special hits. Extended burn applied by ultimates.
ELECTRIC Proc: 40% · Unlocked by default
Physical — Chain Lightning: 0.35× ATK primary hit, chains to up to 3 targets within 3 tiles. Each jump loses 20% power.
Special — Static Field: 0.7× SP.ATK to all enemies in 3.0-tile radius. High stun proc rate.
Ultimate — EMP: Instantly destroys all enemy shields in zone. Grants all zone followers +30 resonance and 1.5× speed for 5 seconds.
Arc Flash proc (40%): Stuns target briefly. Highest proc rate of all elements.
ICE Proc: 8% · Shop: 20 shards
Physical — Frost Lance: 0.55× ATK single-target. Hit chance = 60% + ACC/50 (max 98%). Proc blocked by active shields.
Special — Ice Burst: 0.6× SP.ATK in 2.5-tile radius. Applies slow (40% speed) for 3s. Freeze/slow blocked by shields.
Ultimate — Blizzard: 8s zone-wide freeze field. Every 0.5s: 60% freeze chance on each enemy in zone. Frozen enemies can escape each frame (very slowly).
Hard Freeze proc (8%): Rare but powerful — target fully stops. Cannot freeze shielded enemies.
FLUX Proc: 15% · Shop: 25 shards
Physical — Volatility Aura: 0.6× ATK + disorient proc. Pierces shields — disorient always applies regardless of shield status.
Special — Singularity: Creates a gravity well at target. Pulls enemies within 3 tiles inward (0.8-tile yank). Deals 0.5× SP.ATK and applies disorient. Pierces shields.
Ultimate — Disorient: All predators in zone enter friendly-fire mode for 4s — they attack each other at 0.5× power.
Disorient proc (15%): Target attacks randomly (including allies) for 2s. Bypasses shields.
CORE Proc: 30% · Shop: 30 shards
Physical — Shield Bash: 0.45× ATK + grants caster a defense boost for 1.5s. Proc knocks target 1.5 tiles back.
Special — Radiant Pulse: Caster becomes invulnerable for 1s. Shields all allies within 3.0 tiles (shield HP = 1.5× SP.ATK). Also deals 0.4× SP.ATK to enemies in range.
Ultimate — Shield Grant: Every follower on the entire map instantly receives a 10 HP shield.
Knockback proc (30%): Physically launches target 1.5 tiles away from attacker.
CORE bonus: CORE followers build pylons instantly — no 30s construction delay.
TOXIC Proc: 50% · Shop: 35 shards
Physical — Smoke Form: Caster enters 75% evasion for ~0.75s while striking for 0.4× ATK. High proc rate shreds target defense.
Special — Toxic Cloud: Drops a 1.8-tile lingering cloud at target. Deals tick damage every 0.33s for ~5s. Persists on the ground.
Ultimate — Smoke Screen: 8s zone-wide fog. All enemies in zone suffer −50% accuracy for the duration.
Defense Shred proc (50%): Cuts target's defense by 50% for 2s. Highest proc rate of all elements.
Recruitment: When an enemy reaches your Crystal it converts into a follower — randomly assigned a personality, element, role, combat trait, natural trait, and perk. Followers carry their element into battle.
ROLES
Role is auto-assigned from the follower's dominant stats at recruitment time.
B
Brawler — Aggressive melee fighter. Chases enemies directly, closes distance fast.
Requires: ATK ≥ 14 (and SP.ATK < 12) Best with: Aggressive or Wild personality · Berserker combat trait
S
Sniper — Ranged attacker. Keeps optimal distance from targets, fires special attacks.
Requires: SP.ATK ≥ 12 OR ACC ≥ 12 Best with: Cunning personality · Opportunistic combat trait
C
Camper — Holds position, defends pylons and chokepoints. Doesn't advance far.
Requires: DEF ≥ 14 OR WILL ≥ 24 Best with: Cautious or Stoic personality · Iron Will perk
H
Hybrid — Flexible offense. Uses both physical and special attacks fluidly.
Requires: ATK ≥ 14 AND SP.ATK ≥ 12 Rarest role. Cunning personality can naturally produce Hybrids.

Each follower rolls one perk, one combat trait, and one natural trait at recruitment. These are permanent and cannot be changed.
PERKS (passive stat modifiers)
Iron Will
All incoming damage is reduced by 25% (multiplied by 0.75 before applying). Best on frontline Brawlers and Campers who take constant hits.
Siphon
Heals for 10% of every point of damage dealt. Self-sustaining on high-damage followers. Pairs extremely well with Berserker combat trait — dealing more damage heals more.
COMBAT TRAITS (active behavior)
Berserker
When the follower's HP drops below 30% of max, all damage output multiplied by 1.5×. Dangerous but volatile — low-HP followers die fast without support. Best with Siphon perk or Aggressive personality.
Opportunistic
Automatically re-targets the lowest-health enemy rather than the nearest. Excellent for finishing off wounded enemies and snowballing kills. Pairs well with Snipers who have the range to pick targets.
NATURAL TRAITS (idle behavior)
Empathetic
Prefers staying near other allied followers when idle. Creates natural clustering — good for coordinated pushes and mutual defense. Empathetic followers are easier to keep together.
Lone Wolf
When idle (no combat orders), wanders up to 4 tiles away from the player on its own. Can patrol or scout but may stray into danger. Harder to keep in formation.
RESONANCE SYSTEM
RESONANCE (0–100) builds during combat: +3 per hit, +20 per kill.
At 100, triggers network buffs and resets. Wild personality starts with 1.5× resonance multiplier — fills fastest.
Electric ultimate grants +30 resonance instantly to all followers in zone.
5 species across 4 ranks. Each zone spawns a species based on night number — front zones escalate first. Every enemy killed drops shards and DNA splices. Bosses drop Crystal Modulators.

SPAWN ESCALATION
Rank upgrades cascade from the front zone backward, one zone per night threshold.
Night 6+: front zones become Beetle. Night 11+: Scorpion. Night 16+: Spider.
Each night beyond the threshold converts one more zone to the new rank.
Enemies to kill: 3 + (Night−1) × 2 per night (Night 1: 3 kills, Night 5: 11 kills, Night 10: 21 kills).
Pylons are your primary defensive structures. Build them during the day, defend them at night. Their power multiplies when grouped into networks of the same element.

PYLON MODES
Attack Mode: A pylon with an assigned element acts as a defensive tower. Automatically fires at enemies within its detection range (~2.5 tiles). Each element fires its own attack type.
Wave Mode: Place 2 adjacent pylons of the same element — they pair up automatically and project a zone-effect corridor between them. The zone type matches the element.
Tip: CORE pylons forming an enclosed triangle create a protected zone. Flux solo pylons emit an inward-pulling glow ring around them.
NETWORK STRENGTH
Same-element pylons within 5 tiles auto-connect. The largest connected group determines your network tier.
T1 2+ pylons — basic network bonus active
T2 4+ pylons — enhanced zone effects + ultimate charge +15% faster
T3 6+ pylons — maximum network power, strongest zone projection
Tier-ups trigger a pulse flash on all connected pylons in the network.
NETWORK INTEGRITY
Range0 – 100. Shown as a percentage per element.
Builds+Tier × 0.5 per frame while network is intact and connected
Decays−2 per frame when connections break or pylons are destroyed

+25% power permanently awarded to any pylon that survives an entire night without being destroyed. Shown visually on the pylon. Carries across all future nights.

ZONE OVERVIEW
The world is divided into zones (15 tiles wide each). Zone 0 is your Home base. Zones 1+ are hostile forward zones with escalating enemies. Control more zones to unlock powerful Armament purchases.
ZONE OBJECTS
▣ NEST
Enemy spawn structure at the center of each forward zone (y=2). 200 HP. Spawns waves of enemies each night.
Destroy — Use the LEFT radial command to assign followers to attack it. Eliminating a nest stops spawns from that zone.
Link to Pylon — Use the RIGHT radial on an adjacent pylon, then select the nest. Enemies from that nest are routed through your pylon network. Fire meteor deals 2× damage to nests.
▦ WALL PANEL
Appear on back walls in forward zones (~18% chance per wall tile). Interact to activate.
Reward Panel (60%) — Awards 5–15 shards on activation.
Decoy Panel (40%) — Triggers an alarm that makes nearby enemies immediately hostile. Three alarm types:
• Proximity — Enemies within 6 tiles become hostile
• Zone — All enemies in the same zone become hostile
• Facility — All enemies on the map become hostile
◉ CAPACITOR NODE
One per forward zone, positioned near the front (zone start + 3 tiles). Capturable by assigning followers with the RIGHT radial command.
Capture grants player territory in that zone and awards +5 shards each wave while held. Counts toward zone control for Armament unlocks.
Capture is interrupted if an enemy enters within 1.5 tiles.
◈ SIGNAL TOWER
Spawns in zones 4 and beyond (zone center, y=1). While uncaptured, grants the enemy enemy territory — increasing enemy push strength in that zone.
Capturing a Signal Tower converts it to player territory and removes the enemy spawn advantage. Counts toward zone control.
◧ MEMORY BANK
A hacked nest node — found in some zones. Capturing it marks the nest zone as player-controlled, enabling you to use it as a forward spawn point for the next wave.
TERRITORY CONTROL
Territory is recalculated every second based on proximity to control points.
Player (blue)Within 3 tiles of a green pylon or captured node
Enemy (red)Within 4 tiles of an active nest or uncaptured signal tower
ContestedOverlapping player and enemy territory — priority combat zone
ZONE CONTROL COUNT
The Armament shop tracks zones controlled: Home (1) + each captured Capacitor Node or Signal Tower. Reach higher counts to unlock powerful elite units:
• 2 zones — Power Boost, Armor Plating
• 3 zones — Elite Vanguard (3 elite followers)
• 4 zones — Strike Force (5 hardened fighters)
• 5 zones — Apex Unit (1 supreme commander)
The Crystal is your base of operations. It sits at the heart of your home zone. If the Crystal's HP reaches zero — Game Over. Protect it at all costs.

RECRUITMENT
When an enemy reaches the Crystal and doesn't immediately attack, it converts into a follower. The conversion assigns:
Random personality (Aggressive / Cautious / Cunning / Stoic / Wild)
Random element from the unlocked element pool
Auto-assigned role (Brawler / Sniper / Camper / Hybrid) based on stats
Random combat trait (Berserker or Opportunistic)
Random natural trait (Empathetic or Lone Wolf)
Random perk (Iron Will or Siphon)
A Crystal Modulator (dropped by bosses) lets you set an active element pair — all newly recruited followers draw from that element pool instead of random.
